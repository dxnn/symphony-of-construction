Generators make notes (letters) and lengths (symbols)
Select then key to add a generator, space to remove
Notes and lengths try to find each other
When they do they make noise and vanish

The url is your save, it updates automatically

Default frequencies are in a major key
Can hack in url but might get weird
Z is always noise

800 lines, no dependencies, single index.html file

The spectrum is also a tempo/volume fader

Cellular automata movement means compositions are deterministic
But overdriven gain sounds different in different browsers
